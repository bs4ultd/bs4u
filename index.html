Who We Are

We perform structural, brickwork, timber/carpentry, electrical and plumbing to a very high standard. We have been trusted construction and engineering company in London as a family business for 15 years. Our aim is to deliver consistent work, on time and on budget time after time. Our construction and engineering team is tried and tested to be very skilled at what they do. Rest assured that we approach all projects assigned as unique and careful attention to detail. We have a solid reputation that is established on years of experience in the design, construction and engineering (electrical/plumbing) industry.

We have a team of highly experienced, reliable, and skilled professionals and project manager making sure all elements are covered. Our team will work together with you as the client on the project, however small or big it may be. We troubleshoot any arising unexpected problems and we outline all our works in details. Our construction and engineering team is tried and tested to be very tested and be very skilled at what they do. Our clients rate us very highly.
